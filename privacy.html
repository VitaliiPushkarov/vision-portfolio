Privacy Policy

Our advisory group places utmost importance on safeguarding your confidential data. This Data Protection Statement delineates the categories of information we gather, its utilization, and the precautions we implement to ensure its safety.

Data We Accumulate

We amass personal details through diverse channels, including website interactions, account registrations, newsletter subscriptions, and participation in our offered services or resources. The collected information encompasses:

Identifiable Personal Data:

  • 1. Full Name
  • 2. Electronic Mail Address
  • 3. Contact Number

Non-Identifiable Technical Data:

  • 1. Web Browser Type
  • 2. Computer Operating System
  • 3. Internet Access Provider
  • 4. Unique IP Address
  • 5. Timestamp of Site Visits
  • 6. Webpages Accessed
  • 7. Duration of Site Engagement

Utilization of Gathered Information

Promptly address client support inquiries and technical assistance needs.

Analyze user engagement patterns to enhance our service offerings based on user feedback.

Facilitate order processing and related service delivery without disclosing information to external entities, except when necessary for service provision.

Maintain communication with users regarding their orders and respond to various inquiries or requests.

Distribute periodic electronic communications to opt-in subscribers, including corporate updates, industry news, and relevant service information.

Digital Tracking Policy

Our advisory group is committed to upholding your privacy and maintaining transparency regarding our technological practices. This Digital Tracking Policy elucidates our use of cookies and similar technologies to optimize your online experience. Our digital platform employs tracking technologies to enhance your browsing session. These are small data files stored on your device by websites you visit. You can adjust your preferences for these technologies through your browser configuration.

Safeguarding Your Information

All sensitive data transmitted online is protected using advanced encryption protocols. Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who are bound by confidentiality agreements. We conduct frequent assessments of our security measures to ensure ongoing data protection.

Information Dissemination

We refrain from selling, trading, or transferring your personal data to external parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist in our business operations and service delivery. We may disclose your information when legally required or to protect our rights, property, or safety. In the event of corporate restructuring, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

External Website Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services beyond our control. This Data Protection Statement does not apply to these external sites, and we bear no responsibility for their privacy practices. You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, widely-used, and machine-readable format. You may object to the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances. You can request the erasure of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations. You may request corrections to any inaccuracies in your personal information.

Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights concerning your personal data, including access, rectification, deletion, or processing restrictions. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Amendments to this Statement

We may modify this Data Protection Statement periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be published on our website, and we encourage regular review of this policy. Significant changes will be communicated via email or through our digital platform.

Get in Touch
